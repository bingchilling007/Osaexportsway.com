Common Questions

Frequently Asked by Buyers

What is the minimum order quantity?
Our minimum order is typically one full reefer container (approx. 20–24 tonnes). For trial orders, we may accommodate smaller quantities — please contact us to discuss your requirements.
How quickly can you fulfil orders?
Standard lead time is 10–21 days from order confirmation, depending on product specifications and current inventory. We maintain buffer stock for key SKUs to support faster turnaround.
Do you offer private-label packaging?
Yes. We offer private-label packaging and custom branding for supermarket chains, hotel groups, and large-volume distributors. Minimum volume requirements apply — contact us for details.
What payment terms do you accept?
We accept Letter of Credit (LC at sight), Telegraphic Transfer (TT), and for established buyers, other mutually agreed arrangements. Payment terms are confirmed during the order process.
Can you provide product samples?
We provide product samples for qualified buyers. Sample shipments are arranged via air freight at buyer's cost. Please describe your specification requirements when requesting samples.
Which incoterms do you use?
We most commonly trade on FOB (Indian port), CFR, and CIF basis. We can accommodate buyer's preferred incoterm where possible — your export manager will confirm during negotiation.
