🐑 Lamb Meat

Premium Halal Lamb — Export Quality

Our lamb is sourced from carefully selected farms across prime livestock regions, where animals are raised on natural pastures under strict veterinary supervision. The meat is characterised by its tender texture, light pink colour, and mild, delicate flavour that is highly sought after by Gulf consumers.

Slaughtered under certified Islamic halal protocols, processed in hygienically controlled facilities, and vacuum-packed immediately after cutting, our lamb maintains optimal freshness through transit.

Available FormsWhole Carcass, Bone-In Cuts, Boneless, Minced
Weight Range8–18 kg per animal (custom grading available)
PackagingVacuum-Seal Bags, Carton Boxes (10–20 kg)
TemperatureChilled 0–4°C / Frozen -18°C and below
Shelf LifeChilled: 21 days | Frozen: 12 months
CertificationsHalal, HACCP, ISO 22000, APEDA

🐐 Goat Meat

Premium Halal Goat — Gulf Market Favourite

Goat meat is a staple across Gulf cuisines and commands consistently high demand during religious festivals including Eid Al-Adha and Eid Al-Fitr. OSA Exports Way maintains year-round supply capacity to meet both standard and festival-season demand surges.

Our goat is lean, naturally flavourful and nutritious — sourced from young animals at optimal weight for the best eating quality. Full carcass, saddle cuts, legs, shoulders, and minced options are available to suit your market's preferences.

Available FormsFull Carcass, Saddle, Legs, Shoulders, Minced
Weight Range10–25 kg per animal (young & adult grades)
PackagingIndividual Wrapping, Vacuum-Seal, Bulk Carton
TemperatureChilled 0–4°C / Frozen -18°C and below
Shelf LifeChilled: 18 days | Frozen: 12 months
CertificationsHalal, HACCP, ISO 22000, APEDA

🥩 Beef

Premium Halal Beef — Marbled & Export-Ready

Our halal beef range is sourced from quality cattle breeds known for excellent conformation and good marbling characteristics. The meat is deep red in colour with naturally distributed intramuscular fat that delivers superior tenderness, flavour, and juice retention — qualities essential for the hotel, restaurant, and retail sectors in Gulf markets.

We supply all primal and sub-primal cuts, and can provide custom portioning, trimming specifications, and branded packaging solutions for distributors and supermarket chains.

Available FormsPrimal, Sub-Primal, Retail Cuts, Diced, Minced
Key CutsRibeye, Striploin, Tenderloin, Chuck, Brisket
PackagingVacuum-Seal, Modified Atmosphere, Bulk Carton
TemperatureChilled 0–4°C / Frozen -18°C and below
Shelf LifeChilled: 35 days (MAP) | Frozen: 18 months
CertificationsHalal, HACCP, ISO 22000, APEDA
