Our Story

About OSA Exports Way

Our Mission

Built on Trust, Delivered with Excellence

OSA Exports Way Pvt Limited was established with a singular vision: to be the most reliable and quality-driven halal meat exporter supplying the Gulf region. Over 12+ years, that vision has shaped every aspect of our operation — from how we select our livestock partners to how we seal every export package.

We are committed to delivering products that meet — and exceed — the dietary, religious, and quality expectations of our import partners. Our relationships with buyers in UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Oman, and Kuwait are built on consistency, transparency, and trust.

Our Journey

A Decade of Growth & Excellence

2012
Foundation & First Exports
OSA Exports Way Pvt Limited was incorporated with a focus on supplying halal-certified lamb and goat meat to UAE distributors. The company's first consignment of 20 tonnes established the standard for quality that we uphold today.
2015
HACCP Certification & Cold Chain Investment
Achieved HACCP certification and invested in state-of-the-art refrigerated logistics infrastructure. Expanded operations to Saudi Arabia and Qatar as buyer confidence grew through our commitment to hygiene and safety standards.
2018
ISO 22000 Accreditation & Beef Range Launch
Secured ISO 22000 Food Safety Management accreditation. Launched our premium halal beef product line, responding to growing demand from hotel and restaurant distributors across the Gulf. Monthly export volumes exceeded 200 tonnes.
2021
Expansion to Oman & Kuwait
Established trading relationships with key importers in Oman and Kuwait, completing our GCC footprint. Introduced custom packaging and private-label services for regional supermarket chains and hotel groups.
2024
Continued Growth & Digital Expansion
Processing over 500 tonnes monthly across all product categories. Continued investment in technology, traceability systems, and sustainable sourcing protocols. Committed to being the partner of choice for Gulf meat importers for the decades ahead.
What We Stand For

Our Core Values

🕌
Halal Integrity
Every product we export is slaughtered, processed, and packaged in strict compliance with Islamic halal guidelines. We maintain rigorous records to ensure full traceability and authenticity for our buyers and their end consumers.
🎯
Uncompromising Quality
From farm selection to final packaging, quality is non-negotiable at every step. Our in-house quality control team and third-party audits ensure every consignment meets internationally accepted food safety benchmarks.
🤝
Partnership-First
We view every importer and distributor as a long-term partner, not just a customer. Our dedicated export managers provide personalised service, flexible order structures, and reliable delivery timelines to support your business needs.
🌿
Ethical Sourcing
We partner only with farms that maintain humane animal husbandry practices, proper veterinary oversight, and responsible environmental stewardship — because sustainable sourcing is fundamental to long-term quality.
🚢
Logistics Excellence
Our integrated cold chain logistics ensure that the quality standards achieved in processing are perfectly preserved through transport and delivery — whether it's a 10-tonne trial order or a 500-tonne contract.
📊
Transparency
We believe importers deserve complete visibility into their supply chain. We provide full documentation — health certificates, halal certificates, packing lists, and COAs — for every shipment without exception.
