Certifications In Detail

🕌
Halal Certification
Islamic Food Authority Certified
All products are produced under strict Islamic halal protocols, certified by an approved Islamic food authority and accepted across all GCC markets.
Slaughter by trained Muslim slaughtermen with Tasmiyah
Ante-mortem and post-mortem veterinary inspection
No cross-contamination with non-halal products
Halal certificate issued per shipment for customs
🏭
HACCP Accreditation
Hazard Analysis Critical Control Points
Our HACCP plan identifies, evaluates, and controls food safety hazards throughout the entire production process — required for export to all GCC markets.
7-principle HACCP plan across all processing lines
CCPs monitored with automated alert systems
Regular internal audits and third-party HACCP verification
All staff HACCP-trained and food safety certified
📋
ISO 22000:2018
Food Safety Management System
Integrates HACCP principles with ISO management requirements. Our certification demonstrates commitment to systematic, internationally recognised food safety practices.
Full FSMS integrated with HACCP controls
Traceability system tracking from animal to export
Annual surveillance audits by accredited certification body
Continuous improvement through management review cycles
✅
PHDEB Registration
Pakistan Halal Development & Export Board
PHDEB registration under Pakistan's Ministry of Commerce is mandatory for all halal meat exporters — providing buyers with government-backed halal compliance assurance.
Registered halal meat exporter under PHDEB framework
Official health certificates via PHDEB-approved veterinarians
Access to Pakistan's internationally recognised halal inspection regime
🧪
Laboratory Testing & QC
Third-Party Accredited Testing
Every batch undergoes comprehensive microbiological and chemical testing at PSQCA-accredited third-party laboratories, with a Certificate of Analysis provided for every shipment.
TVC, E. coli, Salmonella, and Listeria testing
Antibiotic residue and heavy metal screening
COA issued per batch and attached to shipping docs
❄️
Cold Chain & Hygiene
End-to-End Temperature Compliance
Our cold chain infrastructure meets GCC/EU guidelines for refrigerated meat transport. Facility sanitation operates under SSOP verified by internal and external inspectors.
Processing at 8–10°C; packaging room at 4°C
Pre-inspected reefer containers pre-cooled before loading
Digital time-temperature logs provided per shipment
