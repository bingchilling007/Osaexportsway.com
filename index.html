Who We Are

Trusted Partner for International Meat Procurement

Founded in 2012, OSA Exports Way Pvt Limited has grown into one of Pakistan's most respected halal meat export companies. We combine traditional livestock expertise with modern cold-chain logistics to deliver consistently premium products to importers and distributors across the Gulf Cooperation Council.

Our vertically integrated supply chain — from farm selection and veterinary checks to processing, packaging, and refrigerated shipping — ensures that every consignment meets the highest standards expected by Gulf market buyers.
